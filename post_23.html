Something I've never gotten used to — and genuinely hope I never do. 🎤

The moment a girl realizes she's good at this.

Not "maybe someday if I keep practicing." Right now. Today. In this room. She is good at this.

You can see it on her face. It's like something clicks. Like a door opens that she didn't know was there. And all of a sudden she is standing a little taller and singing a little louder and looking out at the room like she belongs here — because she does.

That moment is what I work toward with every single student. Every rehearsal, every performance, every "let's try that again." It's all for that moment.

And it never, ever gets old.
