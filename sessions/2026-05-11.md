## What We Did

### 1. Market Analysis & Revenue Projections (Strategy)

**3-County Target Market:**
- Duval: 1,055,159 | St. Johns: 348,336 | Clay: 238,605 | Total: ~1,642,100
- Elementary-age girls (~4.5%): ~69,790
- 1,000 students = 1.4% market penetration — extremely low bar

**Price Point Decision:** $75/month recommended
- 10-month school year model (August–May)
- Costume fees go to vendor — NOT Miss Stephanie's revenue
- At $75/mo: 107 students needed to hit $80K/year

**High-Throughput Location Model:**
- 3–4 class sections per semester per location = 300–500 students per location
- 8 locations × 500 students × $75/mo × 10 months = $30M/year
- At that scale: performing arts centers, not studios. Multiple directors, staggered times.

**5-Year Expansion:**
- Phase 1 (NE FL, Years 1–3): 8–14 locations → $13M–$30M/year
- Phase 2 (All FL, Years 3–6): 50–80 locations → $46M–$75M/year
- Phase 3 (SE US, Years 6–10): ~400 locations, ~50,000 students → $375M system revenue
- Royalty model at Phase 3: ~$30M/year for Miss Stephanie as franchisor

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Jacksonville Children's Chorus: audition-based, $900+/yr, already at 1,000 students — proves demand
- No direct competitor to Singers Company's model: non-competitive, girls-only, suburban, $75/mo
- Singers Company is a national franchise — proven curriculum, brand recognition
