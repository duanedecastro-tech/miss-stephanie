## CHANNEL STRATEGY

### Schools — Peachjar Only (No Per-School Paid Ads)
Per-school bulletin advertising runs $200–300/school. At 12 priority schools that's $2,400–3,600 — 5× the marketing budget. Not worth it.
**Peachjar handles digital school reach for ~$150–299 total. Three runs:**
- **Run 1: This week** — catch last weeks of school
- **Run 2: Late July/Early August** — back-to-school window
- **Run 3: September** — first month of school, catch latecomers
*Note: Peachjar does NOT distribute during summer. School calendar only.*

### Churches — Primary Channel (100+ within 30 miles)
Free, values-aligned, trusted voice, high conversion. The church pastor or women's ministry director recommending Singers Company is worth more than any paid ad.

**The church performance strategy:** Don't just send a flyer. Miss Stephanie offers a free 10-minute mini-showcase — the girls perform live during a Sunday service or family event. Parents see it. They sign their daughters up on the spot.

**Target radius:** Clay County + Jacksonville Southside + Northern St. Johns = 100+ churches

### Digital Channels — Free First, Paid Second
Build free presence everywhere before spending a dollar on ads.
