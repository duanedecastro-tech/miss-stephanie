I want to tell you something about the girls I've taught over the years. 💜

The ones who came in the quietest — convinced they couldn't sing, convinced they didn't belong anywhere near a stage — those are always the ones who surprise everyone.

Including themselves.

That transformation doesn't happen because of raw talent. It happens because someone believed in them before they believed in themselves. It happens because they found a room where they were safe to try and safe to fail and safe to grow.

That's what I'm building in Middleburg. And enrollment opens in 8 days.

✨ Enrollment opens June 15. Classes start August 15.
