Can we talk about the parents for a second? 💜

Because one of the things I love most about what we do is watching the moms and dads in the audience. The moment their daughter steps onto that stage — their whole face changes.

It's not just pride (though there's plenty of that). It's something deeper. It's watching your child become someone who knows what she's capable of. Someone who didn't think she could do this — and then did it anyway, in front of everyone.

That moment is yours too. We're just the ones who help her get there.
